Anti-Pest Mesh Installation

Protect your home from unwanted pests with our professional anti-pest mesh installation service. Our high-quality mesh creates a physical barrier that prevents birds, possums, rodents, and other pests from nesting in your roof cavity while still allowing proper ventilation.

Benefits of Anti-Pest Mesh

Installing anti-pest mesh offers numerous benefits, including preventing damage to your roof structure, insulation, and electrical wiring from pest activity. It also helps reduce health risks associated with pest infestations and eliminates the need for toxic pest control chemicals. In Queensland's climate, it's an essential protection for your home.

Our Installation Process

Our experienced team will carefully assess your roof to determine the best approach for installation. We use high-grade stainless steel or aluminum mesh that's built to withstand Queensland's harsh climate. The mesh is securely fitted to prevent any gaps where pests might enter, while ensuring proper airflow and ventilation for your roof cavity.
